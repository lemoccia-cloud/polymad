How it works

From forecast to trade in seconds

1

Fetch live Polymarket contracts

polyMad pulls all active weather, sports, and politics markets from Polymarket's API in real time.

2

Run ECMWF ensemble models

Each contract is matched to the nearest forecast grid point. The 51-member ensemble gives a true probability distribution, not just a point estimate.

3

Calculate edge

Edge = model probability − market implied probability. Positive edge means the market is underpricing the event.

4

Get your alerts

Opportunities above your edge threshold surface as alerts with Kelly-sized position recommendations. Act fast — edges close quickly.
